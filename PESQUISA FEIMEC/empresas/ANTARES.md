## O que a empresa faz em 80/20

Em termos práticos, sua oferta gira em torno de Equipamentos e componentes industriais, Soluções de processo, Tecnologias de apoio à produção. O valor central para o cliente está em ganhar eficiência, reduzir parada e retrabalho, acelerar resultado operacional.

## Sinais públicos capturados
Antares Couplings is one of the most important coupling manufacturers in Brazil. A pioneering company in the manufacture of Flexible Couplings in the country, Antares has diversified its line and today offers the same quality in Gear, Elastic and Blade models. The most demanding sectors of Brazilian industry prefer the Antares Couplings brand, including sugar and alcohol, machinery and equipment, mining, steel, paper and cellulose, and oil, proving that quality and respect for the company’s most solid principles, reputable relationships and mutual gains build true alliances.
