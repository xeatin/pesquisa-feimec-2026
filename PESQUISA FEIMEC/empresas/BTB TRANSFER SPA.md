## Sinais públicos capturados
BTB Transfer is a company with over 30 years of innovation, specializing in the manufacture of high-precision transfer machines. Since the creation of its first BB1 transfer machine in 1987, BTB has made significant progress in technological development, combining the best machine tool technologies available on the market. Its expertise includes dedicated lines for the automotive, hydraulic, petrochemical, valves and fittings, as well as household appliances and the locks and keys sector.
